FAQ

Common questions

What types of regulatory pages can PagePulse monitor?

PagePulse can monitor any publicly accessible web page. We ship with 50+ pre-built templates for common regulatory sources including FDA guidance pages, SEC releases, CFPB bulletins, state attorney general advisories, EPA regulations, and more. You can also add any custom URL.

How is this different from Visualping or other web monitoring tools?

Generic tools show you that a page changed. PagePulse tells you what changed, why it matters for compliance, and which regulations or sectors are affected. Plus, our audit-ready change logs are built for examiner reviews — not just email notifications.

How does AI change summarization work?

When a monitored page changes, our AI analyzes the before-and-after content and generates a plain-language summary focused on regulatory impact. Instead of reading through dense legal text, you get a concise explanation of what changed and which compliance areas are affected.

Is the change log suitable for regulatory audits?

Yes. Every change is logged with a precise timestamp, full before/after snapshots, and AI-generated summaries. Logs are exportable as PDF or CSV and include tamper-evident checksums. Many compliance teams use these logs as evidence of their monitoring program.
